About Us

Whether you're living in Tokyo, visiting Kyoto, or exploring anywhere in Japan, our community brings together Exonians from all graduating classes.

We organize casual meetups, professional networking events, and help connect alumni with resources for living and working in Japan—from real estate tips to HR guidance.

Non sibi. Not for oneself. Our network embodies this spirit by helping fellow Exonians navigate life in Japan.

Riken Summer Program

Each summer, select junior-year Exeter students participate in research internships at RIKEN, Japan's largest comprehensive research institution.

This prestigious program gives students hands-on laboratory experience alongside world-class scientists in fields ranging from physics to biology.

For Current Students

  • Applications typically open in early spring
  • Speak with the Science Department for details
  • Connect with program alumni through our network

For Alumni Mentors

If you're a Riken program alum or work in research in Japan, consider mentoring current students. Get in touch to learn more.
