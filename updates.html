Welcome to Exonians in Japan!

We're excited to launch this community page for Phillips Exeter Academy alumni living in, working in, or visiting Japan.

Our goal is simple: help Exonians connect with each other, share experiences, and support one another in navigating life in Japan.

Whether you're here for a few days or have made Japan your home, we'd love to have you join us. Sign up on the main page to stay in the loop!
